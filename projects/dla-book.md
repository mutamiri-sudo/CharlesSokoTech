# "$38 Billion Opportunity" — Published Book

## Overview
*A Beginner's Guide to Winning DLA Contracts*

Authored and published a comprehensive guide translating complex government procurement processes into a clear, actionable framework for businesses looking to win Defense Logistics Agency contracts.

## Why It Matters
The DLA manages over $38 billion in annual procurement. This book makes the process accessible to small and mid-size businesses that have been locked out of government contracting due to complexity.

## Product Thinking Applied
This book is a product — it demonstrates end-to-end ownership from concept to market:
- Market research and opportunity sizing
- Content strategy and information architecture
- Writing, editing, and production
- Publishing and distribution (Amazon)
- Marketing and audience development
