# NeuroTracker Africa — Affiliate Landing Page & Funnel

**Client:** Tare (launching NeuroTracker cognitive-training platform into African markets)
**Role:** Sole builder — strategy, copy, design, build, deploy, analytics
**Stack:** Next.js · Vercel · Conversion-optimized copy · AI-assisted content production

---

## The Brief

Tare had affiliate access to NeuroTracker (a cognitive-training platform with athletic performance applications) and wanted to launch into the African market — starting with high-performance-sports audiences in South Africa and Nigeria. He needed a landing page that could convert cold traffic from paid social and influencer partnerships into trial sign-ups.

---

## What I Built

- Single-page Next.js landing with above-the-fold benefit hook, social proof, demo video, and clear trial CTA
- AI-assisted copy generation tuned for the target market (different pain points and sports references than the US/EU market)
- Conversion architecture: scroll-triggered CTA repeats, exit-intent capture, mobile-first layout (African markets skew mobile)
- Viral video kit — short-form scripts Tare's team can shoot themselves to drive top-of-funnel traffic
- Vercel deploy with edge caching for low latency to African users

---

## Outcome

🔗 **Live:** https://neurotrackerx-landing.vercel.app

Page is in market, driving traffic through Tare's paid and organic channels. Lightweight engagement — I built, he runs.
