# NeuroTracker — Affiliate Landing Page & Funnel

**Client:** Tare (affiliate for NeuroTracker cognitive-training platform)
**Role:** Sole builder — strategy, copy, design, build, deploy, analytics
**Stack:** Next.js · Vercel · Conversion-optimized copy · AI-assisted content production

---

## The Brief

Tare had affiliate access to NeuroTracker — a cognitive-training platform with strong applications in athletic performance and executive focus — and wanted a landing page that could convert cold traffic from paid social and influencer partnerships into trial sign-ups.

---

## What I Built

- Single-page Next.js landing with above-the-fold benefit hook, social proof, demo video, and clear trial CTA
- AI-assisted copy generation tuned for high-performance sports and executive performance audiences
- Conversion architecture: scroll-triggered CTA repeats, exit-intent capture, mobile-first layout
- Viral video kit — short-form scripts Tare's team can shoot themselves to drive top-of-funnel traffic
- Vercel deploy with edge caching for fast global load times
