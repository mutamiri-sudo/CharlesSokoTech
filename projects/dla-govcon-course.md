## The Opportunity

The Defense Logistics Agency awards roughly **$38 billion/year** in contracts, and a meaningful slice goes to small businesses — but the on-ramp is brutal. SAM registration, CAGE codes, DLA Internet Bid Board System (DIBBS), solicitation response, past-performance building — every step is a potential stop sign for a founder without a procurement background.

I wrote the book to demystify that on-ramp. The course is the productized version — a step-by-step path from "never bid on a federal contract" to "submitted and won first DLA award."
